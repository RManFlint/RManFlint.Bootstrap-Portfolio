About Me

Hello, I'm Jim Holmberg, a double grad of University of Kansas with degrees in finance (minor in econ) and law. I worked as an analyst for the Kansas City Federal Reserve Bank for two years before returning for law school.

After law school, I took a position with Edward J. Bellen and Partners, a law firm in Frankfurt am Main, West Germany specializing in military law, to wit, courts-martial and administrative separation proceedings. I lived there for three years and gained passable German language skills.

Upon returning to the United States, I focused my practice on bankruptcy law and general civil litigation, even arguing before the Kansas Supreme Court.
